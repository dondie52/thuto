# Product Marketing Context

*Last updated: 2026-05-16*

## Product Overview
**One-liner:** Thuto helps Botswana students check what their BGCSE results may qualify them for and build a clearer university shortlist.
**What it does:** Thuto calculates best-six BGCSE points, compares them with local programme requirements, and helps students explore institutions, modules, careers, deadlines, saved programmes, and side-by-side comparisons. Guidance is indicative and must be confirmed with official university sources.
**Product category:** Botswana university planning and admissions guidance tool.
**Product type:** Progressive Web App.
**Business model:** Free-first student utility. Premium account features are planned for saved choices, alerts, and richer guidance.

## Target Audience
**Target companies:** Not B2B. The main users are individual Botswana students and their supporters.
**Decision-makers:** Students choosing programmes, with parents, guardians, teachers, and sponsors as secondary influencers.
**Primary use case:** Check likely programme eligibility from BGCSE grades before applying.
**Jobs to be done:**
- Find out which programmes are realistic with current or estimated grades.
- Compare requirements without opening many university sites.
- Build a shortlist and know what to confirm with institutions.
**Use cases:**
- A student enters grades to see likely, close, and not eligible programmes.
- A parent helps compare fees, duration, careers, and application windows.
- A student saves or compares programmes before visiting official university sites.

## Personas
| Persona | Cares about | Challenge | Value we promise |
|---------|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| First-time student | Knowing what options are realistic | Requirements feel scattered and confusing | A calm first check from grades to possible programmes |
| Parent or supporter | Helping the student avoid missed deadlines or bad choices | Hard to compare institutions quickly | Clear programme facts and reminders to confirm officially |
| Focused applicant | Shortlisting and comparing options | Too many tabs, PDFs, and repeated checks | Saved choices, compare views, and quick return paths |

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Students often do not know which Botswana programmes their BGCSE points and subject grades may qualify them for.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- University websites and PDFs can be hard to compare side by side.
- Requirements may be spread across prospectuses, programme pages, and admissions notes.
- Generic search does not connect grades to programme eligibility.
**What it costs them:** Time, confusion, missed options, and weaker preparation before applications.
**Emotional tension:** Students want reassurance, but they also need honest guidance that does not overpromise admission.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Manual university website and prospectus checking - accurate but slow and fragmented.
**Secondary:** Search engines and social media advice - easy to access but inconsistent and not personalized to grades.
**Indirect:** Asking teachers, friends, or admissions offices - useful but not always available at the moment of exploration.

## Differentiation
**Key differentiators:**
- BGCSE best-six calculation tied to programme exploration.
- Local Botswana institution and programme data in one PWA.
- Offline-friendly, free-first guidance with saved and compare flows.
**How we do it differently:** Thuto turns grades into a planning view, then points students toward official confirmation.
**Why that's better:** Students can move from uncertainty to a shortlist faster while staying aware that final admissions decisions belong to institutions.
**Why customers choose us:** It is practical, local, calm, and built around the actual first question: "What might I qualify for?"

## Objections
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| Is this official? | No. Thuto is planning guidance. Confirm final requirements, fees, and deadlines with each institution. |
| What if my grades are not final? | Use estimated grades to explore early, then update them later. |
| Is my data safe? | Core predictor and saved choices can stay local on the device unless optional account or community features are configured. |

**Anti-persona:** Anyone who needs an official admissions decision, guaranteed acceptance, or direct application processing inside Thuto.

## Switching Dynamics
**Push:** Students are tired of guessing from scattered websites and PDFs.
**Pull:** A quick eligibility check gives immediate clarity and a next step.
**Habit:** Many students rely on word of mouth or search because that is familiar.
**Anxiety:** They may worry the app is wrong, unofficial, or too much work to start.

## Customer Language
**How they describe the problem:**
- "What can I study with my points?"
- "Do I qualify for this programme?"
- "Which university should I apply to?"
- "I do not want to miss a requirement."
**How they describe us:**
- "A place to check my points and options."
- "A Botswana university companion."
**Words to use:** check, may qualify, points, requirements, shortlist, compare, confirm, official source.
**Words to avoid:** guaranteed, accepted, approved, official admission, instant placement.
**Glossary:**
| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| BGCSE | Botswana General Certificate of Secondary Education |
| Best-six | The six highest scoring subjects used for the points total |
| Indicative | Useful for planning, but not an official decision |
